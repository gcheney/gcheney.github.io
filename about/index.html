In my spare time I can be found working on new projects at Github, making new music on Soundcloud, playing guitar with my amp turned up to 11, or listening to podcasts. A few of my favorites include Hardcore History, Planet Money, 99% Invisible and Radiolab. While I still enjoy creating music and shredding some guitar, I am currently focused on my new found passion for programming, software development and the web. You can keep up with my latest work here and read my thoughts on programming and code here on my blog.
