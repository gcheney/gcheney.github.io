Hey there, I'm Glendon (Glen) Cheney

I'm a full-stack web developer and film composer living in Austin, Texas.

Currently, I work at WP Engine. In my spare time I can be found working on new projects at Github, making new music on Soundcloud, or doing coding challenges on HackerRank.

After graduating from Berklee College of Music, I spent four years living and working in Los Angeles as a film composer, and had the opportunity to work on quite a few interesting projects. While I still enjoy creating music, I am currently focused on my new found passion for programming, software development and the web. You can keep up with my latest work here and read my thoughts on programming and code at my blog.
